Maintenance Best Practices

Daily Grease Checks

Empty grease cups daily to prevent overflow and potential fire hazards. A clean cup is your first line of defense.

Filter Maintenance

Run baffle filters through the dishwasher nightly. Carbon buildup reduces airflow and increases energy costs.

Fan Belt Inspection

Listen for squealing sounds from the rooftop fan. This often indicates a worn belt that needs immediate replacement.

Lighting Maintenance

Ensure vapor-proof globes are clean and intact. Proper lighting is essential for safe cooking and inspection.
