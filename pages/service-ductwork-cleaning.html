Why Ductwork Cleaning Is Critical

Fire Prevention

Grease buildup in ductwork is a leading cause of restaurant fires. Regular cleaning eliminates this fire hazard.

System Efficiency

Clean ducts improve airflow and ventilation performance, reducing energy costs and improving kitchen air quality.

Code Compliance

NFPA 96 requires regular ductwork cleaning. Stay compliant and avoid fines or insurance issues.
