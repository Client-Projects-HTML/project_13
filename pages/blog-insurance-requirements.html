Compliance Insurance

If you file a fire claim and can't prove you've been cleaning your exhaust system, your insurance carrier may deny your claim. Understanding your policy's specific cleaning requirements is vital for protecting your business assets.

Common Policy Clauses

  • Proof of Performance: Carriers often require certificates from a third-party professional cleaner as proof of maintenance.
  • Frequency Adherence: You must follow NFPA 96 cleaning frequency guidelines appropriate for your cooking volume.
  • Access to All Areas: Cleaning must cover the complete system (hood, duct, fan). "Hood only" cleaning is insufficient.
