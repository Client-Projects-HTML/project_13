Frequently Asked Questions

According to NFPA 96, commercial kitchen exhaust systems should be cleaned based on cooking volume. Heavy cooking areas may require monthly cleaning, while moderate use kitchens should clean every 3 months. Light use facilities can often go 6 months between cleanings.
